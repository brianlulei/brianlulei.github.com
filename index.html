Lei Lu

Short Bio

Lei Lu is a Senior Member of Technical Staff at VMware Inc. He got his Ph.D. in Computer Science at the College of William and Mary in 2014. Before that, he received his Master and Bachelor degrees from Nanjing University. He spent his first 18 years in Tianjin attending Tianjin Nankai High School.

Lei is interested in doing research in operating systems, virtualization, resource management, and performance analysis.
